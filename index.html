I am an international multi-award winning creator of stories. I am currently focusing on design thinking, user experience and process in collaboration. I design and create projects that focus on human and communication. I like to solve and spot the problems by learning behaviours to make solutions.

I am also an Artist + a Photographer where I doing my practice in core principle with human(social) interaction in architecture. Find out more about me.
